Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering your custom rope halter

How long does it take to make my custom halter? +

Each custom rope halter takes approximately 1-2 weeks to complete from the time we receive your order and measurements. During busy periods, this may extend to 3 weeks. We'll keep you updated throughout the process!

What measurements do you need for my horse? +

We need the nose circumference, head circumference behind the ears, and the distance from behind the ears to the nose. Detailed measuring instructions will be provided with your order confirmation. Don't worry - we're here to help if you need guidance!

What sizes do you offer? +

We offer standard sizes (Mini/foal, Pony, Cob, Full, X-Full,)

Do you ship internationally? +

Currently, we ship within South Africa at a flat rate of R100 using either PUDO or Postnet to Postnet. International shipping may be available for an additional fee ‚Äì please contact us to discuss your specific location and shipping needs.
